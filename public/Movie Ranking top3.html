Best Movies to watch from Carlos' view

My go to list on a rainy day

Scott Pilgrim VS the World

This movie is already a cult classic. I highly recommed the movie as it's artistic with it's clever use of CGI. It's witty banter always gets a chuckle out of me.

The Batman

This one made it feel like a true Batman movie. The scenes were amazingly shot. All thanks to the keen eye of Matt Reeves. If only I can experience this movie for the first time again.

The Wild Robot

Pixar really knocked it out of the part with this one. But then again I really am an animal lover at heart. It was beautiful to say the least. Definitely a good family movie.
